About us
Bazm-e-BME connects Biomedical Engineering students, researchers, and professionals across Pakistan to advance the field. We focus on spreading positivity, fostering collaboration, and driving research and development. With 1,250+ members, we're building a strong network to transform healthcare and medical engineering in Pakistan and beyond.
