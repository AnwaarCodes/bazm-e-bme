The Future Investor:

Harnessing Innovation Amid Volatility

October 1, 2024

HALL Arts Hotel | 1717 Leonard Street | Dallas, TX

REGISTER

About Us

Numerous catalysts and black swan events continue to alter today’s financial world. Historic elections, geo-political tensions, major climate catastrophes, high interest rates and revolutionary technologies are just a few of the factors that continue to transform how investors approach their craft. Overall, the environment for investing is simultaneously daunting yet extremely accessible.

During this afternoon briefing, we’ll hear from seasoned industry leaders who are tasked with maneuvering volatility in order to offer high-quality goods and services. Professionals from various sectors will provide insights on new methodologies, rising challenges, and recent successes within their respective fields. We’ll also hear about the inner workings of industry giants about the strategies employed for leveraging the power of technology. Ultimately, how can big data and AI be used to spur financial innovations and steer crucial business decisions?

Additionally, what’s the right mix of human and machine when it comes to managing finance? How do we navigate the fine line between algorithms and analytics and human intelligence and insight? And how can both Wall Street veterans and retail investors uncover new opportunities?

Together, we will explore and learn about a future where data not only plays a pivotal role in investment decisions but serves as a driving force for innovative, investable enterprises.
