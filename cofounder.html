# Message from Founder & Co-Founder

: " We Rise by Lifting Others" ~ Robert G. Ingersoll Biomedical Engineering is an interdisciplinary field in which we integrate knowledge of Physics, Life Sciences, Chemistry, and Mathematics to design, develop, and improve healthcare facilities. Unfortunately, the Biomedical Engineering Field is not fully functional in Pakistan and people are unaware of its true essence i.e. creativity and innovation. As the students of Biomedical Engineering, it was very painful for us. That is why, Bazm-e-BME, a society trying to uplift the Biomedical Engineering field in Pakistan, was established. Bazm-e-BME is working on the "Bae-Khudi" Philosophy of Allama Muhammad Iqbal, the national poet of the East, who advocated for selflessness and service to humanity. From the universities of Karachi to the hospitals of Kashmir, we all Biomedical Engineers and BME Students are united by the knot of Bazm-e-BME. We are trying to facilitate others without personal benefits. We are working with enthusiasm and perseverance just to reduce our people's suffering. Through Bazm, we are spreading the message of creativity, innovation, and collaboration for designing a future where learning becomes a lifestyle. . Your support and trust matters. Let’s collaborate, learn, explore, and grow together for Biomedical Engineering. Through our mutual efforts, the fragrance of BME will surely spread in every corner of Pakistan and it will be the source of merriment for every individual in our country.
